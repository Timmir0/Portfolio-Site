About

Nathan Boles is a Baltimore based Game Designer, Programmer, and Writer, who has been developing both digital and tabletop games since 2018. Nathan uses experimentation, innovation, and iteration to find the fun in the games they make. A tired perfectionist who is skilled at paying attention to the smallest of details to make an experience stand out, they take great pride in their work. In 2024, they had their work presented in both MICA and University of Baltimore, receiving positive reception.
